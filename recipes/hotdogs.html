Hot Dogs

Description

As simple as cooking a hot dog can be, there's nothing like the satisfaction of watching the meat cook over a flame.
Ingredients
  • Hot Dog
  • Hot Dog Bun
  • Any condiment you'd like
    • Ketchup
    • Mustard
    • Relish
Steps
  1. Put a hotdog on a stick or utensil and roast it over the fire to your desired look.
  2. Take the hot dog and put it insided the hotdog bun.
  3. Top your hot dog with any of the condiments listed above, or if there's a different one you'd prefer.
  4. Enjoy!
