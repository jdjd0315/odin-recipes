Vegetables

Description

Even when you're camping, you still need to have some healthy food in your life. This is the recipe to make that happen.
Ingredients
  • Tin Foil
  • Olive Oil
  • Any vegetable you'd like
    • Corn on the cob
    • Diced potatoes
    • Zucchinis
    • Onions
    • Carrots
Steps
  1. Roll out 3 layers of tin foil to create a sturdy base.
  2. Lightly drizzle olive oil onto the tin foil.
  3. Lay the vegetables in a single layer on the tin foil.
  4. Wrap up the vegetables with the tin foil tightly.
  5. Enjoy!
