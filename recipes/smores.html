Smores

Description

Regardless what season it may be, there is nothing like roasting smores with your family. All it takes are a few ingredients, a flame, and some sticks to make a memory!
Ingredients
  • Graham Crackers
  • Marshmallows
  • Chocolate Bars
Steps
  1. Put a marshmallow (or two) on a stick and roast it to your desired look.
  2. Take a graham cracker and break it in half
  3. Treat the graham crackers like two pieces of bread, the marshmallow and chocolate bars like meat, and make a sandwich.
  4. Enjoy!
